How we got started


Many years ago, a stray Alsation puppy arrived on our doorstep. We couldn't find anybody that owned the poor little guy so we took him in and called him Catch. Ever since that day, we've have been collecting dogs, cats and any other animals that need our help.


We all need a place to call home and that includes domesticated animals, big and small.

Our Services


Not only do we offer homes to animals, we also offer a rehoming service, Veterinary care and charity events.


We're proud to run our pet rescue centre and thank you all for your support.

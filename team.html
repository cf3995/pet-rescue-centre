How we got started

Many years ago, a stray Alsation puppy arrived on our doorstep. We couldn't find anybody that owned the poor little guy so we took him in and called him Catch. Ever since that day, we've have been collecting dogs and cats that need our help.

Our Services

Not only do we rescue animals, we also offer a rehoming service, Veterinary care and charity events. We're proud to run our pet rescue centre and thank you all for your support.
